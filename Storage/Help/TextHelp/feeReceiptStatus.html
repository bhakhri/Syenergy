The different filters below allow you to choose the criteria based on which you want to see the fees information. For example if you wish to see the fees of students who are doing B.Tech and have paid fees between period X and Y, then use the "Degree" and "From Date...To Date" filters below to specify the values and click "Show list".

If on the other hand you wish to see the list of all students who have paid fees for a particular "Fees cycle" then choose the "Fees Cycle" drop down to specify a particular value.

In this way, this extremely versatile fees filter can be used to select your particular criteria for a fees report and then show the details of fees paid as per that criteria.

From the fees listing then, you can use the detail buttons to drill down and look at the details of the particular transaction.
